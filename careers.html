Immersive Xperience Developer

AR/VR | Web 3D | XR | Unity/Unreal | Full Stack (React/NestJS)
Location: Remote or Hybrid  |  Type: Full-Time  |  Experience: Beginner to Intermediate

About the Role

We’re looking for a passionate and driven Immersive Xperience Developer to join our growing team. You'll work on immersive and interactive applications spanning AR, VR, and 3D web technologies. If you're comfortable building across the stack — from front-end UIs to backend services — and have a strong foundation in immersive technologies and collaboration, we’d love to meet you.

What You’ll Do

  • Work with Unreal Engine or Unity on AR/VR experiences.
  • Build Web 3D/XR apps with Three.js or React Three Fiber.
  • Develop scalable backends using NestJS.
  • Follow Git workflows, issue tracking, and best practices.
  • Work cross-functionally with design and product teams.
  • Optimize for performance and cross-device XR support.
  • Help the team overcome creative/technical obstacles.

What We’re Looking For

  • Experience with React or Angular.
  • Familiarity with NestJS or similar frameworks.
  • Unity or Unreal Engine experience in XR contexts.
  • Comfort with Web 3D tools like React Three Fiber.
  • Experience with browser-based or native AR/VR.
  • Git + issue tracking (Jira, GitHub) workflows.
  • Strong communication and team collaboration.

Bonus Skills

  • Interest or background in Generative AI/ML.
  • Mobile dev with React Native or similar tools.
  • Understanding of spatial computing or computer vision basics.
  • Docker and containerized dev workflows.

Why Join Us?

  • Work at the cutting edge of art and tech.
  • Collaborate on immersive, meaningful projects.
  • Flexible, open culture with room to grow.

Apply if You...

  • Love interactive 3D and immersive tech.
  • Want to shape the future of XR.
  • Thrive in collaborative, fast-moving teams.
